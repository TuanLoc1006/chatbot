=== Military academies, universities and colleges ===
{{Main|Vietnamese military academies}}Military academies and universitites are professionally managed by the Ministry of National Defense. The Minister of National Defense sets admission standards for each academy such as candidate's background, entry health, quota,... Meanwhile, the Ministry of Education and Training supports the academies. organize and operate teaching and management activities, as well as on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense, receive input enrollment documents and manage output graduation records.
